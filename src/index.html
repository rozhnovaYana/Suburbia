About Us
Suburbia Studio -- is a Ukrainian 3D rendering studio.
We create high-end photorealistic 3D renders, VR Tours, and animations for interiors, exteriors, and products.

Suburbia Studio was founded in 2018 by Julian Sadokha. In total, for this time our team has completed successfully more than 92 projects from all around the world. You can witness them in Sydney, Melbourne, Paris, Berlin, New York, London, Dubai, and other cities.

We are proud of our creative work which exceeds expectations, as well as of long-lasting relationships with our clients. Our team strives to create beautiful images and help our customers to achieve their goals. This is why we pay a lot of attention to our service and every little detail.
